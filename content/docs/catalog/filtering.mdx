When the catalog has more than a handful of items, filters are how you find what you need fast. The filter bar at the top combines a free-text search with three dropdown filters and a clear-filters button.

## Search

The search box does a loose text match against the name of every family and
collection. Type two or three characters — the grid updates without you pressing
enter. Case doesn't matter.

## Brand

Every item in the catalog belongs to a brand. Use the **Brand** dropdown to
show only one brand's inventory. This is the fastest way to page through
everything that belongs to, say, your events team.

## Category

Categories are a shorthand for what a family is — *Event Chairs*, *LED
Screens*, *Backdrop Panels*, and so on. Mixing a category filter with a brand
filter is the fastest way to get to a specific shape of item.

## Team / Department

If your tenant has teams or departments configured, a **Team** filter shows up
alongside the other dropdowns. Teams let you tag inventory that belongs to one
part of your company — the *Marketing* team's inventory vs the *Events* team's.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="No team filter visible?">
The Team filter only appears when at least one item in the catalog is assigned
to a team. If nothing is tagged, the filter stays hidden to keep the bar clean.
</Callout>

## Clear filters

Once you've applied any filter, a **Clear filters** button appears next to the
view tabs. Clicking it resets everything in one go — search, all three
dropdowns, and the view tab.
