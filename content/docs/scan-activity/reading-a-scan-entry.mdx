## Top row — badges

Every card opens with two badges:

- **Scan type badge** on the left. One of six types, each with its own
  colour:
    - **Outbound Scan** — primary
    - **Inbound Scan** — secondary
    - **Derig Capture** — purple
    - **Outbound Truck Photos** — blue
    - **Return Truck Photos** — orange
    - **On Site Capture** — pink
- **Condition badge** on the right. Only present when the scan
  captured a condition (GREEN / ORANGE / RED). Capture-only scans
  like truck photos or on-site capture don't carry a condition.

## Main row — asset info

Below the badges:

- **Asset name** in large monospace — e.g. "Event Chair (batch)".
- If the asset has a QR code, a **Print QR** button shows beside the
  name.
- A single sub-line reads: `QR: <code> · Qty: <n> · <tracking-method>`
  — everything the physical item has, at a glance.

## Right column — timestamp + actor

- **Timestamp** in `MMM d, HH:mm` format, your browser's local time
  zone.
- **"by `<name>`"** — who scanned. This is a logistics team member by
  name, not a system user. If a scan is auto-generated, it shows as
  "System".

## Below the main row

Three optional sections, shown only when they have data:

### Notes

A free-text block the scanner typed when recording the event.
Renders in a subtle grey card beneath the main info.

### Discrepancy

An amber "Discrepancy: BROKEN / LOST / OTHER" banner. Only appears on
inbound scans that found something wrong. See
[Discrepancies](../scan-activity/discrepancies) for the full story.

### Photos

A 3-column grid of every photo attached to the scan. Click any to
open the lightbox. See
[Photos and the lightbox](../scan-activity/photos-and-the-lightbox).
