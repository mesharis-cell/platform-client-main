Your administrator provisions every portal account. Once they've added you, you'll have an email address and password you can use to sign in.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- The tenant URL your admin shared with you (for example,
  <code>https://demo.kadence.test</code>).
- The email address registered on your account.
- Your password. If you don't have one yet, or you've forgotten it, the portal
  can send you a verification code to reset.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="Accounts are admin-provisioned">
You can't self-register. If you don't see a login that works, ask your
administrator to create an account for you.
</Callout>

## Sign in

<StepList>
    <Step n={1} title="Open the tenant URL">
        Visit the portal URL your admin shared. You'll land on the login screen —
        the grid-patterned page titled <strong>Access / Control</strong>.
    </Step>
    <Step n={2} title="Enter your email and password">
        Type the email address your admin registered. If you type it in mixed
        case, the portal will lowercase it for you before submitting.
    </Step>
    <Step n={3} title="Click Grant Access">
        The button changes to <em>Authenticating…</em> while the portal verifies
        your credentials. A success toast reading <strong>Access Granted</strong>
        appears on correct login, and you're forwarded to your dashboard.
    </Step>
</StepList>

Click <strong>Forgot Password →</strong> at the bottom of the login form.
You'll receive a 6-digit verification code by email; enter it along with a
new password (minimum 8 characters).
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

- **&quot;Authentication Failed&quot; toast.** Double-check the email is
  the one your admin registered. Passwords are case-sensitive.
- **&quot;Access Denied&quot; toast.** The account exists, but the role isn't
  <code>CLIENT</code>. If you need access, ask your admin to check the role
  on your profile.
- **Nothing happens when you click Grant Access.** A flaky network
  connection is the usual cause. Reload the page and try again.
