If you can't sign in — maybe this is your first time, maybe you forgot — the portal has a self-serve reset that sends a one-time code to your email. No admin help needed.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- **Access to the email inbox** on file for your account. Codes arrive
  within a minute from the portal's sender domain.
- **Access to the login page.** Start at the portal URL and click
  <PortalLink to="/forgot-password" label="Forgot Password" /> at the
  bottom of the form.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="Codes expire quickly">
The one-time code is valid for **5 minutes**. If it times out, request a
new one with the **Resend** button on step 2.
</Callout>

        From the login screen, click **Forgot Password →**. The page

    <Step n={2} title="Enter your account email">
        Type the exact email address registered on your account. Click
        **Continue**.
    </Step>
    <Step n={3} title="Watch for the confirmation toast">
        A toast reading **OTP Sent / A verification code has been sent to
        your email** appears. The page transitions to step 2 — the code
        entry form.
    </Step>
</StepList>

<Callout kind="tip" title="Check spam if the email doesn't arrive">
Most inboxes deliver the code in under a minute. If you don't see it,
look in spam or clutter folders before asking your admin.
</Callout>

<StepList>
    <Step n={1} title="Type or paste the six-digit code">
        The code arrives as digits only. If you mis-type, the **Reset
        Password** button stays disabled until all six slots are filled.
    </Step>
    <Step n={2} title="Set your new password">
        Minimum **8 characters**. Use the eye icon to unhide what
        you're typing. Fill both the *New Password* and *Confirm Password*
        fields — they must match.
    </Step>
    <Step n={3} title="Click Reset Password">
        On success you'll see a **Password Reset Successful** toast and
        the portal redirects you to the login screen. Sign in with your
        new password to finish.
    </Step>
</StepList>

<Callout kind="tip" title="Need a new code?">
If you hit the 5-minute expiry, click **Resend** on the code-entry page.
A fresh code lands in your inbox; the old one stops working.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

- **"Reset Failed" toast on submit.** Most commonly this means the code
  has expired. Click **Resend** and try again with the new code.
- **Code never arrives.** Check spam, then confirm the email address you
  entered in step 1 is the exact one on your account. If it is, ask your
  admin to verify the email on file.
- **"Weak Password" toast.** The password is shorter than 8 characters.
  Lengthen it and resubmit.
- **"Password Mismatch" toast.** *New Password* and *Confirm Password*
  don't match. Re-type them.
- **You changed your mind mid-flow.** Click **Change Email** on step 2
  to go back to the email step and start over with a different address.
