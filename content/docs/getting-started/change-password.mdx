When you're already signed in and want to change your password — maybe on a schedule, maybe because you suspect it's been exposed — the portal has a dedicated **Reset Password** page. Different path from the forgot-password flow: this one's authenticated, no email code needed.

## When to use this vs Forgot Password

- **Use this page** when you know your current password and just want to
  change it.
- **Use the <PortalLink to="/forgot-password" label="Forgot Password" />
  flow** when you can't sign in at all.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="You'll be signed out after the change">
    Changing your password invalidates your current session. The portal signs you out automatically
    and takes you to the login page, where you sign in again with the new password.
</Callout>

<StepList>
    <Step n={1} title="Open the dropdown on your avatar">
        In the bottom-left of the sidebar, click the avatar initial. A menu opens with your name,
        **Reset Password**, and **Sign Out**.
    </Step>
    <Step n={2} title="Choose Reset Password">
        Takes you to the authenticated reset page, titled **Change Password**.
    </Step>
    <Step n={3} title="Fill the three fields">
        *Current Password* authenticates you. *New Password* and *Confirm Password* together set the
        change. The portal shows a red **Passwords do not match** hint in real time if the last two
        fields drift apart.
    </Step>
    <Step n={4} title="Click Update Password">
        You'll see a **Password Changed** toast, the portal signs you out, and you're redirected to
        the login screen. Sign back in with the new password to continue.
    </Step>
</StepList>

## Troubleshooting

- **"Password Mismatch" toast.** _New Password_ and _Confirm Password_
  don't match. Re-type to fix.
- **"Weak Password" toast.** The new password is shorter than 8
  characters.
- **"Same Password" toast.** The new password equals the current one.
  Pick something different.
- **"Change Failed" toast with a message about current password.** The
  _Current Password_ field is wrong. Double-check it; if you genuinely
  don't remember it, sign out and use the
    <PortalLink to="/forgot-password" label="Forgot Password" /> flow instead.
