A starter checklist for the most common problems before reaching out to your platform admin.

## I can't log in

- **Wrong email?** Double-check you're using the exact address your
  admin registered. Mixed case is fine — the portal lowercases it
  automatically — but typos in the domain or missing characters
  aren't.
- **Wrong password?** Watch for Caps Lock. Try the show-password eye
  icon to confirm what you typed.
- **"Access Denied" toast?** The account exists but the role isn't
  CLIENT. Ask your admin to fix the role.
- **Nothing happens on Grant Access?** Flaky network is the usual
  cause. Reload the page and try again. If persistent, clear browser
  cache for the portal URL.
- **Lost password?** Use the
  [Forgot Password flow](../getting-started/forgot-password).

## I approved the quote but the order is still QUOTED

Refresh the page. The portal doesn't auto-update — if the approval
went through, a reload will show the CONFIRMED hero and the PO on the
header.

If it's still QUOTED after a hard refresh, the approval didn't land.
Check your browser console for errors and try again.

## The cart is empty when I expected items in it

Two things to check:

- **Different device?** The cart lives in your browser's localStorage —
  it doesn't sync between devices or even between browsers on the same
  device. If you're on a different laptop, you'll see an empty cart.
- **Cleared browser data?** If you recently cleared cookies / site
  data, the cart gets wiped too.

Rebuild the cart from the
[catalog](../catalog/browsing).

## I can't advance past a checkout step

The Continue button stays disabled until every required field on the
current step is valid.

- **Step 2:** Both event dates required; end must be ≥ start; event
  start must satisfy the minimum lead time.
- **Step 3:** Venue name, city, and address required.
- **Step 4:** Contact name, phone (valid format), and email (valid
  format) required.
- **RED items?** You'll also need the
  [feasibility check](../ordering/red-items-and-feasibility) to pass.

A toast usually tells you what's missing if you click Continue on a
step with validation issues.

## I'm stuck on Step 5 — "Submit Order" disabled

Three common reasons:

- **Unresolved ORANGE decisions.** Open the
  [Maintenance Decision Center](../ordering/maintenance-decisions)
  and choose Fix / Use-as-is for every ORANGE item.
- **Availability issues.** An item's availability dropped between
  adding to cart and getting to review. A banner on the Review step
  lists the issues — click "Return to Catalog" to fix.
- **RED feasibility not yet checked.** If your cart has RED items,
  go back to Step 2 and click Continue to run the feasibility check
  first.

## The quote seems wrong

Before assuming a pricing bug, check:

- **Volume.** The **Picking & Handling (X.XXX m³)** line scales with
  your cart's total volume. Adding or removing items between your
  original cart and the quoted cart changes this line.
- **Delivery window.** A tight or unusual delivery window can trigger
  extra transport charges.
- **Permit coordination.** If you flagged permit requirements, permit
  line items might have been added.
- **Reskins / rebrands.** Custom work is priced separately on its own
  line.

If none of that explains the number, ask your admin to decline the
quote and re-quote — they can annotate the specific line you're
asking about.

## My order hasn't changed status in days

First, refresh the page. Then check:

- **Linked service requests** with "blocks_fulfillment" — they can
  hold the order from progressing until they complete. See
  [Linked service requests](../order-page/linked-service-requests).
- **Event date is far out?** Prep usually starts a few days before
  event start, not immediately on approval.

If the status genuinely seems stuck, email the logistics team — the
order ID is all they need to look it up.

## I didn't get an email I expected

- **Spam folder first.** Most delivery issues resolve there.
- **Correct email on file?** Profile edits aren't self-serve; ask
  your admin to verify the email address on your account.
- **Event that doesn't send email?** Some transitions (Order Ready
  for Delivery, Order Closed, your own quote approval) don't email
  you. See [Emails you receive](../reference/emails-you-receive)
  for the complete list.

## A feature I read about isn't visible

Some features are gated per-company. Service Requests, New Stock
Request, Event Calendar, Self-Pickup, and Kadence Invoicing all
depend on platform-level feature flags for your tenant.

If a link or nav item isn't showing up, your company likely doesn't
have that flag enabled. Ask your admin to turn it on.

## Still stuck?

Email your platform admin with:

- The order ID (or service request ID, inbound request ID) if relevant.
- A screenshot of the issue.
- What you tried already.

That's almost always enough to resolve it on the first round-trip.
