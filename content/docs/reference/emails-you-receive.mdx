The platform uses email as the main push channel — the portal itself doesn't notify you in-app. This page is the complete list of emails you'll receive across the lifecycle of an order, service request, or self-pickup.

## Order-lifecycle emails

### Order Submitted

- **Subject:** `Order Submitted: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** Immediately after you submit the order.
- **What to do:** Nothing. You'll receive the next email when logistics
  has a quote ready.

### Quote Ready

- **Subject:** `Quote Ready: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** After logistics finishes pricing your order.
- **What to do:** Open the order. Review the quote. Approve (with a PO
  number) or decline. See
  [Approving with a PO number](../quotes/approving-with-a-po-number).

### Revised Quote

- **Subject:** `Revised Quote: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** If logistics revises the original quote.
- **What to do:** Read the revision reason in the email, then go back
  to the portal to approve the new quote.

### Order Confirmed

- **Subject:** `Order Confirmed: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** Immediately after you approve the quote.
- **What to do:** Nothing. Prep starts.

### Order In Transit

- **Subject:** `Order In Transit: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** When the delivery truck dispatches.
- **What to do:** Make sure someone is at the venue to receive.

### Order Delivered

- **Subject:** `Order Delivered: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** When items arrive at the venue and are signed for.
- **What to do:** Enjoy the event. Check the scan activity if you want
  to see arrival photos.

### Pickup Reminder — 48 Hours

- **Subject:** `Pickup Reminder: {ORDER_ID} — 48 Hours`
- **When:** Two days before scheduled pickup.
- **What to do:** Have items ready at the scheduled time and location.

### Delivery / Pickup Times Updated

- **Subject:** `Delivery/Pickup Times Updated: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** If logistics adjusts your delivery or pickup window.
- **What to do:** Review the new time in the portal.

### Invoice Ready

- **Subject:** `Invoice Ready: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** When the platform issues the final invoice.
- **What to do:** Download the PDF from the email or the order page.

### Order Cancelled

- **Subject:** `Order Cancelled: {ORDER_ID}`
- **When:** Only if an admin cancels your order.
- **What to do:** Read the reason in the email. Contact your admin if
  you didn't expect it.

## Service request emails

Only arrive if service requests are enabled for your company.

- **Service Request Quote Ready** — review and approve/decline the
  quote for service work.
- **Service Request Quote Revised** — if the quote changes.
- **Service Request Complete** — work is done.
- **Invoice Ready** — service request invoice issued.

## Inbound request emails

Only if your company uses the stock-request feature.

- **Inbound Request Submitted** — confirmation.
- **Cost Estimate Ready** — review and approve/decline.
- **Stock Intake Complete** — items added to your inventory.
- **Invoice Ready** — inbound request invoice issued.

## Account emails

- **Password Reset** — OTP code during the forgot-password flow. Code
  expires in 5 minutes. See
  [Resetting a forgotten password](../getting-started/forgot-password).

## Which emails you won't get

Some events happen without a client email because they're routine or
internal — you won't get a notification for:

- Order Ready for Delivery (intermediate status; you get the "In
  Transit" email instead).
- Order Closed (the order just quietly finishes).
- Quote Approved / Declined on your own orders (you already know —
  you did it).

<Callout kind="tip" title="Every email links back to the portal">
    Every transactional email the platform sends includes a direct link to the order, service
    request, or self-pickup detail page. Click through when you need to take an action.
</Callout>
