The checkout flow is short but opinionated — it asks for everything it needs up-front so the logistics team has no follow-ups. A small amount of prep before you click Proceed to Checkout makes the rest go fast.

## Have this ready

- **Items in your cart.** At least one. Review them at
    <PortalLink to="/catalog" label="the catalog" /> or open the floating cart to verify.
- **Event start and end dates.** The portal enforces a lead time (usually
  24 hours, sometimes longer) between the moment you submit and the
  earliest event start it will accept. If you're cutting it fine, have
  the numbers picked before you start.
- **Venue name, address, city, and country.**
- **An on-site contact.** Phone number and email — this is the person
  the logistics team will actually call on delivery day.
- **Permit info if your venue needs them.** Who's arranging them
  (you, the platform, or still being worked out), whether vehicle docs
  or staff IDs are needed, and any notes the team should know.
- **A decision on every ORANGE item** in your cart. The checkout will
  block you on the Review step until each one has a "Fix before event"
  or "Use as-is" choice. More on that in the
  [Maintenance decisions article](../ordering/maintenance-decisions).

<Callout kind="tip" title="Your cart persists">
    You don't have to submit in one sitting. The cart survives refreshes and browser restarts, and
    the checkout form itself is saved per-step — if you bounce out partway, you'll land back on the
    same step with your inputs preserved when you return.
</Callout>

## Open the checkout

From the floating cart panel in the bottom-right, click
**Proceed to Checkout**. The portal takes you to

<PortalLink to="/checkout" label="/checkout" /> and mounts the five-step stepper.
