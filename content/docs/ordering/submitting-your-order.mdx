Step 5 is the final read-through before your order hits the logistics team. Everything you typed on Steps 1-4 is summarized on one scrollable page, plus an optional estimate preview and the Submit Order button.

The summary groups your inputs into four cards:

- **Order Items** — every line from your cart with its quantity.
- **Event** — start and end dates, formatted for human reading.
- **Venue** — venue name, city, address, and any permit coordination.
- **Contact** — the person logistics will call on delivery day.

Everything is read-only on this page. If you spot something wrong, use
the **Back** button to step backward and fix it — your inputs are
preserved on every previous step.

## Estimate preview (if enabled)

Some tenants enable an automatic estimate preview on the Review step.
When it's on and your venue city is filled in, you'll see a cost
breakdown before you submit — picking & handling, transport, subtotal,
estimated total.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="The estimate is indicative, not final">
    The number on this page is a pre-quote estimate. The actual quote is generated by the logistics
    team after your order is reviewed and may include line items the automatic estimate can't know
    about — extra services, specific vehicle types, permit charges, rebranding. Use the estimate as
    a ballpark only.
</Callout>

If the estimate can't be calculated (usually because the venue city
isn't in the coverage list), the card shows a friendly fallback: you'll
receive a custom quote by email within 24-48 hours after submitting.

## Submit

Click **Submit Order** when everything looks right. The portal shows
a brief loading state, fires a toast on success — **Order submitted
successfully! Order ID: ORD-YYYYMMDD-NNN** — and takes you to the new
order's detail page where you can track its status from SUBMITTED
onward.

Your cart is cleared automatically on successful submit.
