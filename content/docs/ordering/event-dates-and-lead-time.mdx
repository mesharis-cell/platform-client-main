Step 2 is where your event timing lands in the system. Two dates are required; a preferred delivery window is optional.

**Event Start Date** is when your event begins — the moment the items
need to be set up and ready. **Event End Date** is the last day you
need them.

Both are required to advance to Step 3.

## Lead time

The portal enforces a **minimum lead time** between the submission
moment and the earliest accepted event start. The default is 24 hours,
but some tenants configure longer. The date picker dims any date
earlier than that; the Continue button won't let you proceed with an
out-of-range start date.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="Weekends may be skipped">
    Some tenants exclude weekends from lead-time counting. If the earliest accepted date you see
    looks further out than you expected, weekend exclusion is why.
</Callout>

This is a **request**, not a guarantee. Logistics reviews your
preference and confirms the actual delivery window after your quote is
approved — usually it lands within your preferred window, occasionally
it shifts by a few hours for route reasons.

Leave all three fields empty if you have no preference; the team will
pick a sensible default.
