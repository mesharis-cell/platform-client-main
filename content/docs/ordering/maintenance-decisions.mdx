If any item in your cart is flagged **ORANGE** — minor issues, not ready-as-is — the Review step surfaces a **Maintenance Decision Center** that asks you for a choice per item. You can't submit the order until every ORANGE item has a decision.

For each ORANGE item, you have two options:

### Fix before event

The platform refurbishes the unit between now and your event start
date. Condition notes and refurb-days estimate are used to schedule
the work. Usually the preferred choice.

### Use as-is

The unit ships in its current condition. Pick this when you've seen
the condition notes and decided they don't matter for your event —
common for cosmetic-only flags on back-of-house items.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="You can't skip the decision">
    Until every ORANGE item has a choice, the **Submit Order** button stays disabled. The Review
    step surfaces a counter showing how many items still need a decision.
</Callout>

## Review condition details first

Each ORANGE item's card shows a **View details** link that opens a
modal with:

- The original condition notes
- The refurb-days estimate
- Any condition photos captured during scanning

Use it to check what the flag actually says before you pick a lane.
