Step 3 covers everything logistics needs about the physical location — name, address, access notes, and any permit coordination.

- **Venue Name** is required.
- **Country** is auto-selected to your tenant's default (e.g., UAE)
  and usually shouldn't change.
- **City** lazy-loads the list of cities for the selected country.
  Required.
- **Full Address** is a textarea — one building might have multiple
  entrances, so write enough detail that a driver knows exactly where
  to pull up.

## Venue contact (always visible)

Below the venue fields, an **always-visible** venue contact section
captures the name, email, and phone of someone at the venue who can
coordinate arrival, access, unloading, or handover. This is separate
from the permit block — logistics needs it even when no permits are
involved.

## Permits

Check **"Venue requires permits or access coordination"** if your
venue has any access restriction the platform needs to coordinate.
The checkbox expands a detailed section.

**Permit Owner** picks one of three:

- **"I will arrange"** — you're handling the permits yourself. The
  platform just needs to know.
- **"You should arrange"** — platform staff coordinates with the venue.
- **"Not sure yet"** — you'll come back with an answer.

<Callout kind="tip" title="Additional charges may apply">
    Permit coordination can add to the final cost depending on venue requirements — the quote you
    receive will reflect this. Filling the info in now saves a back-and-forth later.
</Callout>

## Access notes

At the bottom of the step, an **Access Notes** textarea is always
visible regardless of the permit choice. Use it for loading-dock info,
gate access codes, elevator restrictions, and anything a driver or
installer would need to know without a permit being involved.
