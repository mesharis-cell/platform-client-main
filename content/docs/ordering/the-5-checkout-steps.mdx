Every order goes through the same five-step checkout. Most steps are filled in under a minute; Review is the only one where you'll spend real time.

The five steps, in order:

1. **Order Review** — confirm what you're ordering.
2. **Installation Details** — event dates and optional preferred
   delivery window.
3. **Installation Location** — venue info, access notes, and any
   permit coordination.
4. **Execution Contact** — the on-site person logistics will call.
5. **Review** — final check, estimate preview, and Submit.

## Navigating

- **Continue** moves forward. If a required field on the current step
  is empty or invalid, Continue stays disabled and a toast points out
  what's missing.
- **Back** moves one step earlier. Your inputs on the current step
  are preserved.
- **Step labels** are for orientation only — you can't jump forward by
  clicking them. Each step's required fields gate the next.

## Save and resume

The portal persists your checkout state to your browser every time
you type. If you refresh, close the tab, or come back tomorrow, you
land on the step you left and the fields you filled are still there.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="Your cart and form live in the browser">
If you start checkout on your laptop and switch to your phone, you'll
see an empty cart and an empty form. Finish on the device you started
with, or rebuild from scratch.
</Callout>
