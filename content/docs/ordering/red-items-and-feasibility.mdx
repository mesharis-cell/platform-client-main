**RED** items need repair before they can ship. The checkout runs a **maintenance feasibility check** that compares each RED item's refurb-days estimate to your event start date. If the timeline works, the check passes and you can continue. If not, checkout surfaces the earliest feasible date and blocks the step.

## When the check runs

The feasibility check fires on Step 2 (Installation Details) the first
time you try to advance to Step 3, and again on Step 5 just before
final submission. Each run re-reads your current event start date and
the current condition of every RED item in your cart.

When you click Continue, the banner flips to an amber "Checking
maintenance feasibility…" state for a beat. If the refurb timeline
works, you're forwarded straight to Step 3 — a brief green flash
confirms success. No extra work needed.

When the check fails, the portal tells you **exactly** which items
are blocking and what the earliest feasible start date is for each.
You have three options:

1. **Push the event start back** to the latest earliest-feasible date
   among the blockers.
2. **Remove the blocking items** from your cart and add alternatives.
3. **Talk to your admin** — there may be a faster refurb path for a
   specific unit.

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="Feasibility re-runs on every date change">
If you adjust the Event Start Date, click Continue again to re-check.
The indicator goes back to "Checking…" for a beat, then lands on
passed or failed with the fresh data.
</Callout>
