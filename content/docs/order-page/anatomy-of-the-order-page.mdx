## The status hero

The large banner at the top is your primary signal. It shows:

- A **status badge** in the order's colour.
- A **large headline** that summarises the current stage in plain English
  ("Quote Ready", "On The Way", "Pickup Scheduled"…). The full mapping
  from internal status to headline is in
  [Status meanings](../order-page/status-meaning).
- A **one-line description** under the headline — what's happening and
  what, if anything, needs to happen next.
- A colour-matched **icon tile** on the right so you can recognise the
  stage at a glance.

Cancelled orders also render a secondary red banner with the cancellation
reason underneath the hero.

## The Order ID card

A small card next to the hero prints the order ID in large monospace and,
once the quote is approved, the **PO number** you entered. This is the
piece you'll copy into emails, support tickets, or internal systems.

## Quote review or summary

Immediately below the hero, the pricing section changes shape based on
the order's status:

- **QUOTED** — renders the [Quote Review section](../quotes/approving-with-a-po-number)
  with Accept / Decline buttons and the PO-number input.
- **APPROVED / DECLINED / CONFIRMED** — renders a **Quote Summary** card
  showing the same pricing breakdown in read-only form, plus a
  timestamped "Approved on DATE" or "Declined on DATE — REASON".

Later in the lifecycle this card stays visible as your record of what
was agreed.

## Items, timeline, and supporting cards

Below the quote area you'll find:

- **Order Items** — one row per asset with quantity, volume, and weight.
  If the order includes any rebranding work, those items show a badge.
- **Status Timeline** — chronological list of every transition the order
  has been through. Covered in
  [Timeline and history](../order-page/timeline-and-history).
- **Linked Service Requests** — visible when your order has service
  requests linked to it. Covered in
  [Linked service requests](../order-page/linked-service-requests).
- **Supporting Documents** — attachments the team or you uploaded
  against the order. Covered in
  [Supporting documents](../order-page/supporting-documents).
- **Scan Activity** — in the fulfillment stages (CONFIRMED through
  CLOSED), a dedicated timeline of every outbound, on-site, and inbound
  scan event. See the Scan Activity tutorials.

## Right sidebar

A persistent sidebar on the right stacks:

- **Download Cost Estimate** — a one-click PDF export once the quote has
  been sent. Available from QUOTED onwards.
- **Event** — start/end dates.
- **Venue** — name, city, address.
- **Contact** — the person you named at checkout Step 4.
- **Special Instructions** — if you wrote any.

## Action footer

Right at the bottom, a link back to **All Orders** plus (for closed
orders) a **Browse Catalog for Next Event** shortcut.
