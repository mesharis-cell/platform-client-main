An order moves through a sequence of statuses from the moment you submit it to the moment it closes out. The portal shows each status three ways: as a coloured badge in the My Orders list, as a large headline on the order page, and as a row on the status timeline. This article maps every one to what's actually happening.

## The lifecycle in order

The happy path runs top-to-bottom:

1. **Submitted → Pricing Review** (automatic, within seconds).
2. **Pricing Review → Quoted** (logistics team action).
3. **Quoted → Confirmed** (you approve the quote with a PO number).
4. **Confirmed → In Preparation → Ready → In Transit → Delivered**
   (logistics + scans).
5. **Delivered → In Use → Awaiting Return → Return in Transit → Closed**
   (during and after your event).

Two off-path states:

- **Declined** — you declined the quote. The order stops there; no
  further lifecycle happens automatically.
- **Cancelled** — an admin cancelled the order (you can't cancel from
  the portal). The status hero shows a destructive banner with the
  cancellation reason.

| Badge                              | Headline             | What it means                                                | What you can do                                                              |
| ---------------------------------- | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **SUBMITTED**                      | Order Submitted      | The portal received your order.                              | Wait — it moves on automatically.                                            |
| **PRICING_REVIEW**                 | Under Review         | Logistics is working out the quote.                          | Wait. You'll get an email when the quote's ready.                            |
| **PENDING_APPROVAL**               | Pricing Under Review | Internal admin review of the draft quote.                    | Wait.                                                                        |
| **QUOTED**                         | Quote Ready          | The team sent you a quote.                                   | [Approve with a PO number](../quotes/approving-with-a-po-number) or decline. |
| **APPROVED**                       | Quote Approved       | You accepted the quote. Preparation kicks off automatically. | Nothing. Status will move to CONFIRMED shortly.                              |
| **DECLINED**                       | Quote Declined       | You declined the quote with a reason.                        | The order stops here. Start a new one if you need to.                        |
| **CONFIRMED**                      | Order Confirmed      | Assets are reserved for you; prep is underway.               | Wait for IN_PREPARATION.                                                     |
| **IN_PREPARATION**                 | Preparing Your Order | Items are being pulled, cleaned, refurbished.                | Wait.                                                                        |
| **READY_FOR_DELIVERY**             | Ready to Ship        | Fully packed, truck being loaded.                            | Wait. You'll be notified at dispatch.                                        |
| **IN_TRANSIT**                     | On The Way           | The truck is on the road.                                    | Make sure someone is at the venue to receive.                                |
| **DELIVERED**                      | Delivered            | Items are at the venue.                                      | Enjoy your event.                                                            |
| **IN_USE** (badge reads "On Site") | On Site              | Event is running.                                            | Nothing scheduled.                                                           |
| **AWAITING_RETURN**                | Pickup Scheduled     | Pickup window is confirmed.                                  | Have items ready for collection at the scheduled time.                       |
| **RETURN_IN_TRANSIT**              | Items Returning      | Truck is on the road back to the warehouse.                  | Wait for CLOSED.                                                             |
| **CLOSED**                         | Order Complete       | Inbound scan done. Everything settled.                       | Nothing. Start your next event.                                              |
| **CANCELLED**                      | — (red banner)       | An admin cancelled. Reason shown on the page.                | Contact your admin if you didn't expect this.                                |

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="You'll usually see PRICING_REVIEW first, not SUBMITTED">
    SUBMITTED is a transient system state. The portal auto-transitions to PRICING_REVIEW within
    seconds, so by the time you see your order page it's almost always showing "Under Review".
</Callout>

## What you see while you wait

Between quote-sent and event day, the order page also shows a **What's
Next** card tailored to the current status — a short explainer that
reads something like _"Your items are being prepared. You'll see them
move to In Transit when the truck dispatches."_ These cards change as
the status moves, so refreshing the page occasionally is a cheap way
to see where you are.
