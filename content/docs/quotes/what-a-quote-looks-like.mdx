When logistics finishes pricing your order, the status hero flips to **Quote Ready**, the platform emails you, and a new **Quote Review** section appears on the order page. This article walks through exactly what shows up.

## The status hero changes

You'll see a large **Quote Ready** headline at the top of the order page with the hero's icon tile in amber. The one-liner underneath reads something like *"Your quote is ready — review the pricing below and either accept with a PO number or decline."*

Below the hero, a dedicated **Quote Review** card renders everything you
need to make a decision:

- A **pricing breakdown** — a line-by-line view of what you're being
  charged for. Covered in detail in the
  [Reading the pricing breakdown article](../quotes/reading-the-pricing-breakdown).
- A **reskin reminder** when the order includes any custom branding
  work (only relevant if your cart had rebrand items — usually none).
- Two buttons: **Accept Quote** and **Decline Quote**.

## The Download Cost Estimate button

A persistent button sits in the right sidebar from the moment the quote is sent onwards: **Download Cost Estimate**. Covered in
[Downloading the cost estimate PDF](../quotes/downloading-the-cost-estimate-pdf).

<Callout kind="heads-up" title="You received an email too">
The same moment your order moves to QUOTED, the platform sends you a
"Quote Ready" email with a direct link back to the order detail page
and (optionally) a cost-estimate PDF download. The email is the signal
to open the portal.
</Callout>
